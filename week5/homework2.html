Minions

Introduction:

The legion of little yellow men are definitely the most "unwatchable" characters. These short-armed, short-legged, banana-loving, cute little yellow people are not only incredibly efficient, they also have an uncanny talent for screwing things up. They have a great ability to act and can cosplay any kind of profession.
